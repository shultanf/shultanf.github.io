Overview

The existence of the Digital Business Study Program (Prodi) at the Faculty of Economics and Business of Universitas Padjadjaran (UNPAD) is a response to global requirements related to the challenges of the 4th industrial revolution. Digital Business undergraduate program focuses on learning about business by utilizing digital technology. This Program’s aims to create graduates who can play a leading role in the era of digital business.

Details

How You Will Earn

Throughout your study, you will experience the learning journey that have been designed to help you enhance your digital business capabilities.
Learning and teaching methods designed in attractive and challenging experience that will include lectures, problem-based learning, case-studies, blended learning, assignments, projects, industry guest lecturer, excursion and examinations. In your final year, you’ll take internship where you will join in a project to several starts up and or companies where you are not only demand to apply your theoretical but which will give you the opportunity to boost your practical knowledge.
Upon graduation, you will have the skills and ability to:
– apply broad theoretical and technical knowledge of business practice in diverse contexts
– provide advice and guidance regarding digital business operations, strategy, leadership, organisation transformation, financial technology, digital marketing, business analytics, cyber security, pitch and negotiation, and UI/UX
– analyses information to make effective business decisions
– data driven decision making
– Data analyst, data mining & insight story teller

Industry Connections

We equip students in learning by doing experience by meet them to the real industry, you will: -undertake and be assessed on activities that allow you to learn, apply and demonstrate your professional practice
-be involved with partner organisations, research center, including industry feedback. These opportunities may be in the form of project-based internship, simulations, or placements.
-Our industry partners include, grab indonesia, sampingan, gojek, telkom indonesia, research center for infomatics, halofina (fintech), amartha (fintech)
